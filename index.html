Now if only we could do something about the 20,000 pets killed by Broward County every year...
Over 20,000 shelter pets are euthanized in Broward County each year at an average cost of $223 per animal to the taxpayer.

Pets’ Broward is a group of compassionate citizens who are committed to ending the yearly killing of thousands of pets in Broward’s public and private shelters through the implementation of the No Kill resolution unanimously passed by the Broward County Commission. To find out what you can do to help end the killing, click Learn More!
